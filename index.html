Our Inspection Services

We offer comprehensive inspection services to meet all your property evaluation needs.

Residential Inspections

Thorough evaluation of single-family homes, condos, and townhouses to identify potential issues before purchase.

Commercial Inspections

Detailed assessment of commercial properties including structural integrity, electrical, plumbing, and HVAC systems.

Pre-Listing Inspections

Identify and address potential issues before listing your property to streamline the selling process.

Mold & Moisture

Specialized inspection to detect mold growth, water damage, and moisture issues that could affect health and structure.

Radon Testing

Professional radon gas testing to ensure your property meets safety standards for this hazardous substance.

Termite & Pest

Inspection for wood-destroying organisms and pest infestations that could compromise your property's integrity.

About Luxvia Inspectors

Founded in 2022, Luxvia inspectors has been providing trusted property inspection services to homeowners, buyers, and real estate professionals throughout the region. Our certified inspectors bring decades of combined experience in construction, engineering, and home inspection.

We pride ourselves on thoroughness, attention to detail, and clear communication. Our comprehensive reports include detailed findings, photographs, and recommendations to help you make informed decisions about your property.
